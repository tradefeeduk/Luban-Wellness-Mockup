The Burning Ritual

How to Burn
Your Frankincense

The Omani ritual of burning frankincense is slow and intentional. Four simple steps are all you need to bring the scent of Dhofar into your home.

1
Light the Charcoal

Place a charcoal disc in your burner and hold a flame to the edge. Wait until it glows orange across the full surface — roughly 60 seconds.

2
Add the Resin

Using tongs, place one or two small pieces of Hojari resin onto the lit charcoal. Smoke will rise gently and the aroma will fill the room.

3
Find Your Measure

A small piece of resin is enough. Add more to intensify the scent. Less is always more — let the ritual breathe.

4
Sit with the Stillness

Breathe slowly and let the scent settle into the room. Allow the calm to find you. This is the tradition of Oman, in your own space.
